Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common inquiries about our tours and services.

How do I book a travel package? expand_more
Booking is easy! Browse our packages page, select your preferred itinerary, and click the "Book This Trip" button. You'll be guided through a secure checkout process. Alternatively, you can contact our agents via the form above for custom arrangements.
What is your refund policy? expand_more
Cancellations made 30 days or more in advance of the travel date will receive a 100% refund. Cancellations made within 15-29 days will receive a 50% refund. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for cancellations made less than 14 days before departure.
Do you offer custom private tours? expand_more
Yes, we specialize in tailor-made private tours. Whether you're traveling solo, as a couple, or in a large group, we can customize itineraries to match your interests, budget, and pace.
Is travel insurance included in the price? expand_more
Travel insurance is not included in our standard package prices. We highly recommend that all travelers purchase comprehensive travel insurance to cover medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost luggage.
Can you help with Japan Rail Pass (JR Pass)? expand_more
Absolutely. We can assist in ordering your JR Pass exchange orders and provide guidance on how to activate and use the pass once you arrive in Japan.
